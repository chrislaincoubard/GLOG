Update : Nov 29, 2022

The Monkeypox 2022 outbreak has to date been detected in 118 countries (either confirmed or suspected cases), with 112 confirming transmission, 6 countries continuing to have suspected cases only, and 33 countries having discarded cases that were suspected, out of which 11 have only discarded cases.
